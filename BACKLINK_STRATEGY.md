## Phase 2: Category-Specific Directories
*Submit specific tool pages to relevant comparison sites.*

### 1. AlternativeTo (High Impact)
**URL:** [alternativeto.net](https://alternativeto.net)
- **Temp Mail:** Submit as an alternative to *Guerrilla Mail*, *10MinuteMail*, and *Temp-Mail.org*.
- **PDF Tools:** Submit as an alternative to *SmallPDF* or *iLovePDF*.
- **Image Tools:** Submit as an alternative to *Canva* (for basic crops/resizes) or *TinyPNG*.

### 2. SaaS Hub / Toolify
**URL:** [saashub.com](https://www.saashub.com) | [toolify.ai](https://www.toolify.ai)
- Category: Productivity & Free AI Utils.

### 3. Developer Directories
- **Dev.to / Hashnode:** Write a "Show & Tell" post explaining how the tools were built (Vite + React).
- **GitHub:** Maintain a public repo for the core logic/packages to gain dev-community backlinks.

## Phase 3: Content Marketing & Social Signals
*Build organic authority through value-driven content.*

### 1. Reddit Strategy
- **r/privacy / r/scams:** Recommend the **Temp Mail** tool for users avoiding spam.
- **r/webdev / r/smallbusiness:** Share the **QR Code Generator** for professional usage.
- **r/students:** Promote **PDF Merge/Split** tools.
- *Rule: Provide value first, link second.*

### 2. Blog & Guest Posting
- **Target Keywords:** "Best free online image resizer", "Safe temporary email 2026", "Merge PDF without uploading".
- **Internal Linking:** Ensure blog posts link directly to the relevant tool pages.

---

## Phase 4: Foundational SEO & Local Business
*Long-term signals for search engines.*

### 1. Social Profiles
- Update profile links on **Twitter (X)**, **LinkedIn**, and **Medium**.
- Create a **Google Business Profile** (optional, if targeting local regions).

### 2. Niche Directories
- **SubmitTo (Various):** Search for "Free web tool directory 2026".
- **Softpedia / FreewareWeb:** Traditional software listing sites that still carry weight.
